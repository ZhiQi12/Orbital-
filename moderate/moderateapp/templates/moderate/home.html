Web Scraping

From Reddit, data from relevant posts and comments are scraped, such as text reviews, number of upvotes and date posted. After cleaning the data, it is passed into the AI model.



Sentiment Analysis

The random forest regressor AI model is used, which is trained on over 200 reddit submissions. The model produces a sentiment score for each review parsed into it. All these scores are consolidated and refined before a final sentiment rating is given by the model.
